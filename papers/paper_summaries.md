# Paper Summaries

## Gururangan et al. (2020)
- Topic: Domain-adaptive pretraining (DAPT)
- Key idea: Continued pretraining on target-domain text improves robustness
- Relevance: Main robustness method explored in this project

## Wang et al. (2022)
- Topic: Robustness and out-of-distribution evaluation in NLP
- Relevance: Motivates cross-dataset evaluation setting
